About Us

Institute of Information Technology & Management is instituted and governed by Mata Leelawati Shikshan in 1999. It is engaged in various philanthropic activities, with Shri T. N. Chaturvedi, a Padma Vibhushan awardee, as the founder President of both, the society and the institute. The institute aims to develop as a Centre of Excellence for imparting technical and management education and generating competent professionals with a high degree of professional acumen, competence and ethical standards. The Institute is centrally located in the “Janakpuri Institutional Area”, which is well connected by public transport Bus and Metro and provides modern infrastructure for sustained teaching learning.
